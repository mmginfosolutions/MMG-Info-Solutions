Welcome to MMG INFO SOLUTIONS,

Simplifying trade & compliance—so you can focus on growth.

🌍 Core Services:

  • Import Export Code (IEC)
    End-to-end management of IEC applications, modifications, and renewals in full compliance with DGFT guidelines.
  • Registration-cum-Membership Certificate (RCMC)
    Streamlined handling of RCMC applications and renewals across export promotion councils.
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
    We provide reliable and secure Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) through authorized Certifying Authorities (CAs). Our services ensure seamless authentication, data integrity, and compliance with regulatory requirements for individuals and businesses.
  • RoDTEP & RoSCTL
    Expertise in filing under RoDTEP and RoSCTL schemes to maximize duty refunds for exporters.
  • Steel Import Monitoring System (SIMS)
    Advanced registration and monitoring support under the Steel Import Monitoring System:
    • Covers import of 284 HS-coded steel items
    • URN generation valid for 75 days with a prescribed fee structure
  • Certificate of Origin (CoO)
    Fast, reliable issuance of CoO via the national e-platform—compliant with FTA and PTA partner requirements.
  • Insurance Broking
    Comprehensive broking and advisory solutions across all insurance verticals—marine cargo, liability, trade credit, and more.
