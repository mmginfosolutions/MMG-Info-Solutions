IEC (Importer Exporter Code)

IEC is a 10-digit code issued by DGFT required for import/export business.

  • Mandatory for trade
  • Lifetime validity
  • Required for customs clearance

RCMC

Registration with Export Promotion Councils to claim incentives and benefits.

Digital Signature

RoDTEP / RoSCTL

Government schemes to refund taxes and improve export competitiveness.

SIMS

Steel Import Monitoring System tracks steel imports and ensures compliance.

Certificate of Origin

Certifies origin of goods for trade agreements and customs clearance.
